What we are?

The Thander Word

We are Thander Word, a family-owned and operated hotel that offers a cozy and comfortable stay in the heart of Karwi. We have been serving our guests with hospitality and warmth since 2010. Our hotel has 20 spacious and well-furnished rooms, equipped with all the modern amenities and facilities. You can choose from standard, deluxe, or suite rooms, depending on your budget and preference. All our rooms have free Wi-Fi, air conditioning, TV, mini fridge, and attached bathrooms.We also have a multi-cuisine restaurant that serves delicious and hygienic food, prepared with fresh and local ingredients. You can enjoy a variety of dishes, from Indian to Chinese to Continental, at our restaurant. We also offer room service, catering, and banquet facilities for your convenience.Our hotel is located in a prime location, close to the main attractions and landmarks of Karwi. You can easily access the railway station, bus stand, market, temple, and other places of interest from our hotel. We also provide car rental, taxi booking, and tour guide services for our guests.Our mission is to make your stay at Hotel Bliss a memorable and enjoyable one. We strive to provide you with the best quality service, comfort, and value for money. We treat every guest as a part of our family and ensure that they feel at home.If you are looking for a friendly, affordable, and relaxing hotel in Karwi, look no further than Hotel Bliss. Contact us today and book your room with us. We look forward to welcoming you soon.
